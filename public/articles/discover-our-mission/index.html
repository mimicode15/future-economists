Welcome!#

Welcome to Future Economists: Where Economics and Innovation Intersect

Researching The Future’s Building Blocks#

Welcome to Future Economists: where we explore the most exciting intersection in the modern world: emerging technologies and their economic impacts.

Right now, it feels like we are experiencing a transformative era in the world’s history. AI is automating jobs and creating new industries, blockchain is reimagining finance, biotechnology is extending lifespans, clean energy is rebuilding infrastructure, and even space is becoming commercial.

So, how will all of this impact the global economy?

Why Tech Economics Matters#

Traditional economics studies factories, trade, and monetary policy. And alongside all of that, today’s economies are driven by:

  • Artificial Intelligence: creating $15+ trillion in economic value by 2030
  • Cryptocurrency: building a $2+ trillion parallel financial system
  • Clean Energy: requiring $150+ trillion in infrastructure investment
  • Biotechnology: enabling a $4+ trillion personalized medicine market

Our Values#

Technology-First Approach#

We analyze how emerging tech creates new markets, disrupts old ones, and redistributes wealth.

Data-Driven Analysis#

We use real market data, industry reports, and economic indicators to form evidence-based insights and opinions.

Student Perspective#

Our articles are written by the generation that will build and lead these tech-driven economies. We know that we need understand what matters because it’s our future.
